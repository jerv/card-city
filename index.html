We value customer service and strive to make your experience with us as simple as possible. We love the communities we serve and we’re proud to support personalities, athletes an organizations that showcase the best California has to offer.

Cardcity wasn’t built in a day

Since we were founded in 2012, we’ve taken our mission of helping our Cardcitizens free up their unused gi card values seriously. We’ve grown beyond our humble beginnings in Fresno to 11 locations throughout California (with more on the way). Along the way, we’ve learned a lot about how to best serve our customers to make converting your gi cards to cash as convenient a process as possible.

Commited to the community

We know we wouldn’t exist without support from the communities we serve so we’re active participants in doing what we can to make the areas we serve vibrant and healthy.
